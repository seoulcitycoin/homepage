1. Introduction

SCITY connects travelers, merchants, and crypto users to build a real-world payments and rewards ecosystem. It aims to evolve beyond memes into practical utility powered by community.

2. Problem

Despite being a top global destination, Seoul lacks crypto-friendly payments and benefits for visitors. A simple payments/rewards infrastructure can stimulate tourism spending and partner participation.

3. Solution: SCITY

  • Wallet/QR-based micropayments PoC
  • Community rewards for content, promotion, translation, guiding
  • Local partnerships with shops/guides/services
  • Multilingual web/app for global access
  • Anti-bot & vesting policies for early stability
