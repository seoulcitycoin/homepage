Seoul City Coin (SCITY) Whitepaper

A Web3 Travel Utility Coin Powered by Seoul's Speed and Spirit

1. Introduction

Seoul City Coin (SCITY) is a blockchain-based utility token designed to serve the digitally advancing global city of Seoul. SCITY offers real-world use cases for tourists, locals, digital nomads, and crypto users who seek both value and experience in one ecosystem.

2. Problem Definition

Although Seoul is a top global travel destination, there is still a lack of user-friendly payment and benefits systems for foreign travelers. In addition, there is limited connection to crypto-based reward models and no direct utility integration with tourism services.

3. SCITY as the Solution

  • QR-based mobile payment experience for travelers
  • Rewards for content contributors and community members
  • Integration with local vendors and tourism services
  • Friendly meme-based branding with cultural relevance
  • Multi-language platforms for global access

4. Token Information

- Token Name: SEOUL CITY COIN
- Symbol: SCITY
- Total Supply: 828,282,828,282
- Distribution: Liquidity, Community Rewards, Marketing, Team
- Network: Polygon (ERC-20 Compatible)

5. Ecosystem Utilities

  • Tour2Earn: Earn tokens by visiting and verifying tourist locations
  • Share2Earn: Get rewarded for reviews, social posts, and videos
  • Guide2Earn: Earn tokens as a travel guide or local support role

6. Roadmap

  • Q3 2025: Smart contract launch / DEX listing / Community building
  • Q4 2025: Partnerships with local tourism services / Pilot programs
  • H1 2026: Mobile app launch / Expansion of real-use merchants
  • H2 2026: NFT utility introduction / Exploring DAO governance

7. Team & Partnerships

Seoul City Coin is built by a team of blockchain experts, designers, and tourism professionals based in Seoul. The project is actively pursuing partnerships with influencers, tourism providers, and global Web3 communities.
